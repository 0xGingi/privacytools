Go Incognito

Go Incognito is a video-based course teaching users of varying experience levels about privacy, security, and anonymity. Go Incognito not only educates users around the world, but it is designed to transform a user’s way of thinking about why, where, when, and how to treat their data and personal information. To top it off, Go Incognito is available free to watch, is shareable to the public through a Creative Commons license, and even offers a premium version for users who want exclusive perks. It’s been in production since May, 2018, and is still in the process of being released. Track its progress here.
