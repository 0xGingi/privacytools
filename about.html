Our Projects

Go Incognito

Go Incognito is a video-based course teaching users of varying experience levels about privacy, security, and anonymity. It’s designed to transform a user’s way of thinking about their data and personal information and how to reclaim it.

Watch Now

Surveillance Report

Surveillance Report centralizes privacy and security news in a digestible format. Not only will listeners learn about the newest updates condensed into a short period of time, but they will also learn how this applies to their daily lives.
